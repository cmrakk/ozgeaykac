Useful Websites!

1. Duolingo
A language learning platform that makes learning a new language both educational and enjoyable through gamified lessons and challenges.

2. TED
A platform featuring a wide range of short, thought-provoking talks on various topics, from science and technology to arts and personal development.

3. Spotify
A music streaming service that offers a vast library of songs, playlists, and podcasts, allowing you to discover new music and create your own playlists.

4. InterNations
A social networking platform for expatriates that provides a space to connect with other people living abroad, join local events, and access resources related to expat life.

5. Meetup
An online platform that helps you find and join local groups and events based on your interests, making it easier to meet like-minded people in your new country.

6. Expat
A website designed specifically for expatriates, offering forums, guides, and resources to help you navigate various aspects of living abroad, from housing to cultural integration.

7. Time Out
An online guide to events, restaurants, attractions, and cultural activities in major cities around the world, helping you discover new experiences in your new home.

8. Couchsurfing
Not just for finding places to stay, Couchsurfing also has a community aspect that lets you connect with locals and fellow travelers, making it a great way to make new friends and explore your surroundings.
